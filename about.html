About Us

Shree Associates is a professionally managed and multidisciplinary group of architects, engineers and construction professionals committed towards exceeding client expectations by providing high quality design consultancy and project management services in a timely, cost effective and professional manner.

Employing about 8 architects, engineers and other technical staff and based at Sehore, Lucknow and Ranchi.

The firm provides Design and Project Management services in Architecture, Engineering and infrastructure sectors to a variety of corporate, government and educational sectors.

Driven by a commitment to sustainability, we integrate environmentally responsible practices into every facet of our work. From energy-efficient designs to eco-friendly materials, we strive to minimize environmental impact while maximizing efficiency and comfort.
